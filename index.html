Use apps to connect your store with external services or create your own integrations using our API.

Take a picture and name your price. You can also import your existing products from for example: Amazon, eBay, legacy e-commerce or bookkeeping system.

2. Sell your product

Sell your product on your webshop. Market your webshop with newsletters, promotions and other selling tools to drive customers to your site. Publish your products on sales channels such as Fyndiq, Tradera, Amazon, eBay and CDON, where they market your products.
